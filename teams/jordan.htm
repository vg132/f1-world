First Pole Position for Jordan was in 1994 at Spa in Belgian, Rubens Barrichello drove the car then. The first win came in the 1998 Belgian GP at Spa with Damon Hill winning the race and Ralf Schumacher in second place, the first time in history that the first victory for a team has been a 1-2 finish.
