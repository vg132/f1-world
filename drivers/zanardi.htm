Nationality Italian

Drivers Championship Wins Best 20th 1993

Zanardi drove Formula One for Lotus. After a crash at Spa 1993 he was not able to drive for 6 months, he then returnd to Lotus and drove some more races for them in 1994. In 1996 he got a chanse to drive Indy Car in USA, Indy Car is almost like Formula One, he won some races and finnished 5 in the championship, his team mate, Jimmy Vasser, won the championship. In 1997 Zanardi was outstanding and won the championship. He also won the championship in 1998. We will see Zanardi back in F1 next season, 1999, with the Williams-Mecachrom F1 team.
