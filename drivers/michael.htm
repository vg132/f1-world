Michael Schumacher

Born Hurth-Hermuhlheim, Germany
Date of Birth 03/01/1969
Grand Prix Starts 119
Grand Prix Wins 33
Pole Positions 20
World Champion 1994 and 1995
F1 Debut Belgium 1991

The best driver that drives today, maybe the best driver of all time(?). M. Schumacher started his Formula 1 career for Jordan in 1991 at the Belgium Grand Prix and he was able to qualify in 7 place(!), although he was not able to complete the race. After the race Benetton hired him for the rest of the season and Benetton driver Roberto Moreno went to drive for Jordan. M. Schumacher scored points in every race he completed, he scored 4 points in his first season, but he only started in 6 races. M. Schumacher won his first race one year after his first F1 start, the Belgium GP in 1992. M. Schumacher stayed at Benetton until 1996, during his time at Benetton he won 19 races and 2 Drivers Championships 1994 and 1995, 1 constructor championship for Benetton-Renault in 1995. M. Schumacher moved to Ferrari for the 1996 season. His salary is about £17,000,000 or $25,000,000. He has won 14 races for Ferrari so far, and more will come.
